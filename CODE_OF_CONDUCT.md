# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Showing empathy and respect.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or public/private abuse.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and public interactions representing the project.

## Enforcement
Report incidents to: `security@seo-tools-platform.local`.
All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
